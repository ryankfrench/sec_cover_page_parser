        <div>ITEM 4.01 Changes in Registrant&#8217;s Certifying Accountant</div>
        <div><br></div>
        <div>On August 3, 2009, Lawrence Scharfman, CPA was dismissed as the Company&#8217;s
Independent Auditor.</div>
        <div><br></div>
        <div>We understand that on August 11, 2009, Lawrence Scharfman, CPA&#8217;s
registration with the Public Companies Accounting Oversight Board, (PCAOB), was
revoked by the PCAOB.</div>
        <div><br></div>
        <div>During the&#160;&#160;Registrant's&#160;&#160;two most recent fiscal years
and during any subsequent
interim&#160;&#160;period&#160;&#160;prior&#160;&#160;to the August 3, 2009,
dismissal as the
Company's&#160;&#160;independent&#160;&#160;auditors,&#160;&#160;there&#160;&#160;were
no&#160;&#160;disagreements&#160;&#160;with Lawrence Scharfman, CPA,
with&#160;&#160;respect&#160;&#160;to&#160;&#160;accounting&#160;&#160;or&#160;&#160;auditing&#160;&#160;issues&#160;&#160;of
the type discussed in Item 304(a)(iv) of Regulation S-K.</div>
        <div>&#160;</div>
        <div>On August 3, 2009, the Company retained Larry O&#8217;Donnell, CPA, PC as the
Company&#8217;s Independent Auditor.</div>
        <div><br></div>
        <div>During the Registrant's two most recent fiscal years or any
subsequent&#160;&#160;interim period prior to engaging Larry O&#8217;Donnell, CPA, PC,
the Company, or someone on the Company's behalf, had not consulted Larry
O&#8217;Donnell, CPA, PC regarding any of the accounting or auditing concerns stated
in Item 304(a)(2) of Regulation S-K.</div>
        <div>&#160;</div>
        <div>On August 17, 2009, the Company provided Lawrence Scharfman, CPA with a
copy of this disclosure and requested that it furnish a letter to the Company,
addressed to the SEC, stating&#160;&#160;that
it&#160;&#160;agreed&#160;&#160;with
the&#160;&#160;statements&#160;&#160;made&#160;&#160;herein or the reasons why
it disagreed.</div>
