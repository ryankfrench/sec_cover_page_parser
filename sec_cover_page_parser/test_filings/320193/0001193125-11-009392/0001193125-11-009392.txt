 <BODY BGCOLOR="WHITE">

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="3"><B>Apple Reports First Quarter Results </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Record Mac, iPhone, iPad Sales Drive Highest
Revenue and Earnings Ever </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Revenue Grows 71 Percent; Earnings Grow 78 Percent </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px;padding-bottom:0px;"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">CUPERTINO, California&#151;January 18, 2011&#151;Apple<FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="1"><SUP
STYLE="vertical-align:baseline; position:relative; bottom:.8ex">&reg;</SUP></FONT> today announced financial results for its fiscal 2011 first quarter ended December&nbsp;25, 2010. The Company posted record revenue of $26.74 billion and record net
quarterly profit of $6 billion, or $6.43 per diluted share. These results compare to revenue of $15.68 billion and net quarterly profit of $3.38 billion, or $3.67 per diluted share, in the year-ago quarter. Gross margin was 38.5 percent compared to
40.9 percent in the year-ago quarter. International sales accounted for 62 percent of the quarter&#146;s revenue. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple sold
4.13&nbsp;million Macs during the quarter, a 23 percent unit increase over the year-ago quarter. The Company sold 16.24&nbsp;million iPhones in the quarter, representing 86 percent unit growth over the year-ago quarter. Apple sold 19.45&nbsp;million
iPods during the quarter, representing a seven percent unit decline from the year-ago quarter. The Company also sold 7.33&nbsp;million iPads during the quarter. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We had a phenomenal holiday quarter with record Mac, iPhone and iPad sales,&#148; said Steve Jobs, Apple&#146;s CEO. &#147;We are firing on all cylinders and we&#146;ve got some exciting things in
the pipeline for this year including iPhone 4 on Verizon which customers can&#146;t wait to get their hands on.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We couldn&#146;t
be happier with the performance of our business, generating $9.8 billion in cash flow from operations during the December quarter,&#148; said Peter Oppenheimer, Apple&#146;s CFO. &#147;Looking ahead to the second fiscal quarter of 2011, we expect
revenue of about $22 billion and we expect diluted earnings per share of about $4.90.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple will provide live streaming of its Q1 2011
