<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On July&nbsp;31, 2019, Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead will be granted restricted stock units for shares of Oracle common stock (the &#147;RSUs&#148;) in accordance with
Oracle&#146;s Amended and Restated 1993 Directors&#146; Stock Plan. The RSUs will represent a pro rata amount of the annual equity award granted to <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-employee</FONT> directors based on the number of complete
calendar months remaining in Oracle&#146;s current fiscal year. The RSUs will fully vest on the first anniversary of the date of grant provided Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead continues to serve as a director on the vest date. Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead will also
receive the standard cash compensation for Oracle&#146;s <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-employee</FONT> directors. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead has entered
into Oracle&#146;s standard form of indemnification agreement pursuant to which Oracle will indemnify her for certain actions taken in her capacity as a director. </P>

<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>TO THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">REDWOOD SHORES, Calif., July&nbsp;10, 2019 &#151; The Oracle Board of Directors today announced that it has unanimously elected Rona A.
Fairhead to the company&#146;s Board of Directors. The election is effective as of July&nbsp;31, 2019 and increases the size of the Board to 15 directors. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;I am very pleased to welcome Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead to the Board,&#148; said Larry Ellison, Chairman of the Board of Directors and Chief
Technology Officer. Bruce Chizen, Chair of the Nomination and Governance Committee, added, &#147;Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead is an accomplished leader with extensive international experience in finance, risk management, government affairs and global
operations. The Board will benefit from her unique perspective.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead, 57, most recently served as Minister of State
for Trade and Export Promotion, Department for International Trade in the United Kingdom from September 2017 to May 2019. She previously served as Chair of the British Broadcasting Corporation Trust (BBC) from 2014 to 2017. From 2006 to 2013,
Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead served as Chair and Chief Executive Officer of the Financial Times Group Limited, which was a division of Pearson plc, and, prior to that, she served as Pearson&#146;s Chief Financial Officer. Before joining Pearson,
Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead held a variety of leadership positions at Bombardier Inc. and Imperial Chemical Industries plc. Mrs.&nbsp;Fairhead previously served as a director of HSBC Holdings plc and PepsiCo, Inc. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Members of Oracle&#146;s Board of Directors serve <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">one-year</FONT> terms and stand for election at the
